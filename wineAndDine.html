DINNING

When in Plakias, Amalthea Restaurant is an exceptional choice for your most memorable journey in Cretan gastronomy.

For our dishes we use fresh fruits and vegetables, extra virgin olive oil, legumes, Cretan honey, local cheeses and high quality meat.

Alianthos Garden Hotel has received the certification of “Greek Breakfast” from the Hellenic Chamber of Hotels. The excellent quality and authenticity of our products and recipes are assured. Our goal is to take you on a gastronomic journey to every corner of our island and discover the unique cultural heritage of our place through flavors and aromas!
