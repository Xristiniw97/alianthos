A MEMORABLE JOURNEY INTO THE CRETAN GASTRONOMY

INDULGE IN LOCAL GASTRONOMY AT AMALTHEA RESTAURANT IN PLAKIAS, CRETE

Combine your vacation to Plakias with a memorable journey into the Cretan gastronomy. Discover Amalthea restaurant in Plakias, Crete and let yourself relax in a friendly ambience while diving deeper into the local cuisine. Whether you're looking for a quick bite before heading off for a night on the town or just a place to satisfy that hunger craving, Amalthea restaurant caters to your needs with a great selection of dishes that please the palate.

EXPERIENCE AUTHENTIC CRETAN CUISINE

Make the most of your holidays to Plakias and get the chance to savour local delicacies and dishes specially prepared for your utmost pleasure by the creative chef Sotiris Diogos and his team of experts. Find a wide range of carefully designed recipes and access a buffet featuring locally sourced meats and fish, frech fruits and vegetables, legumes and cheeses, all prepared with love and extra virgin olive oil.

Accompany your meal with a glass of wine or immerse yourself into the Cretan culture even further and sip a glass of raki, the strong Cretan distilled spirit that warms up your stomach and soul. The restaurant offers a wide variety of desserts and beverages, including traditional homemade liqueurs, coffee and smoothies. Rest assured that quality is a priority, and opt for a unique meal while enjoying your holidays at Alianthos Garden Hotel in Plakias, Crete.

SOAK UP THE FRIENDLY AMBIENCE AT AMALTHEA RESTAURANT IN PLAKIAS, CRETE

Unwind in a friendly atmosphere and fully experience Cretan hospitality. Enjoy your meal while your kids run carefree around the hotel’s premises and kick back with quality services. Consult the hotel’s staff to get personalised recommendations on the daily selections and feel at home while interacting with people ready to put a smile on your face.

Meet the maître d'hôtel, Konstantinos Lazopoulos, who, along with his team, always creates an excellent and welcoming atmosphere in the restaurant, making your satisfaction a priority. Cherish a memorable gastronomical experience and enjoy the perfect mix of the freshest ingredients and centuries-old tradition in the homey atmosphere of Alianthos Garden Hotel in Plakias, Crete.
