FEATURES OF DOUBLE ROOM WITH SEA VIEW

With a soothing decoration thoughtfully implemented to offer you an escape from reality, the double room features a king-size bed and a fully equipped bathroom. The room sleeps up to the three people and is ideal for couples or a family with a child.
