STORY OF ALIANTHOS HOTEL

A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

We are proud to be part of a team that thrives. We have come long way through and we put great effort into building an awarded group of people that are distinguished for their professionalism, devotion and team spirit.

Our values are designated to fulfill our mission: customer-oriented services of high quality, combined with sincere care and friendship, that brings about a top-notch quality holiday.

We continuously invest on our people in order to train them, strengthen them and develop strong bonds with each other as a team. We all work personally as fully responsible professionals deciding on the best of our job at all times and, at the same time, we all work together as part of a team.

If someone ask us about our accomplishments, we will tell him we have still a long way to go and thus we altogether work into improving ourselves every day. We are part of the Cretan culture of hospitality. We comply with this quality, with what “filotimo” means and with our core values that consist of open communication, responsibility, result-oriented workflow and people-centered behavior. We grow each year and we are always looking for exceptional professionals that would be proud as well to become valuable part of our team. We are proud because we have won several awards through the years but we are even more proud of our employees, whom we train and give prominence to their qualities, hard work and achievements.

We all know the name of the people that are staying in the 96 rooms of Alianthos Garden hotel because we want to create a bond of friendship and sincere care. We strive to maintain an enjoyable environment even under the highest-pressure circumstances and our guests return to our hotel for their holidays at every opportunity available.

Our team members are looking forward to the reviews of our customers and this always puts a smile on their faces because this is the reward of a demanding and hard work job of every day. As a verification of the above, please meet our core team professionals to have firsthand a taste of the mentality of our staff and the real sense of our team spirit.
