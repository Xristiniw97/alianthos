STORY OF ALIANTHOS HOTEL

DISCOVER THE STORY OF THE TRADITIONAL FAMILY HOTEL IN PLAKIAS, CRETE

The story of Alianthos Garden Hotel goes back to 1970 when Manolis Vardakis envisioned creating a traditional hotel in Plakias, Crete, that would cater to its guests as if they were family. Since then, Alianthos has been a place dedicated to providing you with excellent services and priceless holiday memories. The Vardakis family decided to invest in facilities and amenities that always put the guests in the centre of attention. The hotel’s staff commits to speaking your language, taking care of your diet, and of course, boosting your mood and appetite for life by keeping the quality standards and values consistently high. At the same time, the hotel’s design and services are constantly taken care of, so that Alianthos Garden Hotel is a modern space and a hotel that feels like home!
