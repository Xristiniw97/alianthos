PLAKIAS CRETE
EXPLORE PLAKIAS, CRETE,
A QUAINT VILLAGE CLOSE
TO RETHYMNO TOWN

Explore Plakias, Crete, a quaint village close to Rethymno Town Discover an authentic Cretan village only a few kilometres away from Rethymno. Travel south into the wild yet astonishing inland to reach the Alianthos Garden Plakias Hotel in Crete.

Marvel at the diverse landscapes and immerse yourself in a natural setting boasting exotic flora and fauna. Access Plakias Beach, a large stretch of coastline a stone’s throw away from the hotel’s premises. Get the chance to familiarise yourself with the surroundings at your own pace by renting a car, visiting historical sites, or strolling to the beaches to swim in crystal-clear azure coastlines.
